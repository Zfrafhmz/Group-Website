ABOUT US

Take a peek inside of the Museum of Illusions Kuala Lumpur!

Welcome to the fascinating world of illusions! Get entertained, confused and completely blown away, experience the unimaginable and learn all about how our wonderful mind works. You’re up for an amazing experience because nothing is what is seems to be at the Museum of Illusions Kuala Lumpur.

You won't believe your eyes!

The Museum of Illusions is a 21st-century museum offering a unique experience that tricks the mind, challenges the vision and makes you question all of your senses.

All exhibits are based on science, mathematics, psychology and art allowing you to learn a lot about vision and the human brain, but in a fun and interactive way!

Best place for edutainment in Kuala Lumpur!

The Museum of Illusions Kuala Lumpur is like no place you’ve visited before. Its both educational and entertaining content will thrill the young and the old. So, no matter if you’re looking for a perfect way to spend some time with your children, an original idea for a date or a unique way to spend quality time with your grandparents – MOI Kuala Lumpur is the answer to all of these questions.
